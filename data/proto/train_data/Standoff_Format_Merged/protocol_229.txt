Phenol extraction of proteins
To 100 l of PBCV-1 in a 1.5 conical plastic centrifuge tube add 150 l of phenol.
Add DTT (~10 l), 10 % SDS (~20 l), 10M LiCl (~15 l) and Ponceau S (~10 l).
Heat 5 min at 75 C, mix thoroughly.
Allow to cool, centrifuge 1 min (5K).
Draw off lower (clear) phase into 1.5 conical plastic centrifuge tube.
Add 1 ml acetone and then 5 l of 1 % Ficoll, mix.
Store 20 min at -20 C.
Centrifuge 1 min (6K).
Discard supernatant, drain tube, wash ppt once with acetone (ppt sticks to tube) and dry.
Dissolve pellet in ~50 l cracking buffer (1 % SDS ; 5 % glycerol ; 0.03125 M Tris-HCl, pH 6.8 ; bromophenol blue-trace).
